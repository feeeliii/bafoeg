Title: Bafög Geschichten

Description: This is a Website that will consist of two parts. First, the user will be able to write About their personal experiences with Bafög. Whether their experience is good or not, this is really just a Collection of real experiences. Second, (in the future) the user will be able to include a "Forderung", meaning a Suggestion on how Bafög could be improved. Both, personal Story and Forderung can be upvoted by other users to Show the most relevant ones first. The Goal is to Display the good and bad sides About Bafög and ways to improve the System. :)
